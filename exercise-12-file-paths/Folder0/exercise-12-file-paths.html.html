All the Animals

Rabbit:

Fluffy with long ears and strong hind legs for hopping. They can be playful and social pets.

Cat:

Independent and often cuddly companions, known for their meticulous grooming and silent steps.

Me:

Loyal to Sandy

Dog:

Loyal and energetic friends, coming in all sizes and with boundless enthusiasm for playtime.

Fish:

Gilled wonders of the water, with fins that propel them through aquatic worlds. Popular as calming companions in dazzling varieties.

Bird:

Feathered singers and acrobats, with wings that carry them through the skies.
